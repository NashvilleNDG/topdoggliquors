## üéØ QUICK CANVA TUTORIAL

### Step-by-Step:

1. **Go to Canva**
   - Visit https://www.canva.com
   - Sign up free or log in

2. **Create Custom Size**
   - Click "Create a design"
   - Click "Custom size"
   - Enter: 1200 x 630 px
   - Click "Create new design"

3. **Add Background**
   - Click "Uploads" on left
   - Upload your store photo
   - Drag to canvas
   - Resize to fill entire canvas

4. **Add Overlay**
   - Click "Elements" on left
   - Search "rectangle"
   - Add black rectangle over image
   - Resize to fill canvas
   - Click rectangle > Transparency: 40-60%

5. **Add Logo**
   - Click "Uploads"
   - Upload logo.png
   - Drag to top-left corner
   - Resize (approx 150-200px wide)

6. **Add Text**
   - Click "Text" on left
   - Add "Heading" for "TOP DOGG LIQUORS"
   - Font: Playfair Display, Bold, 72px
   - Color: White or Gold
   - Center-align

   - Add another text box for subtitle
   - Font: Montserrat, Regular, 36px
   - Text: "Premium Spirits ‚Ä¢ Wine ‚Ä¢ Beer"
   - Color: White
   - Center-align

   - Add text for contact info
   - Font: Montserrat, 24px
   - Include: Address, Phone, Delivery info

7. **Adjust Layout**
   - Ensure text is centered
   - Check 40px margins from edges
   - Verify readability

8. **Download**
   - Click "Share" > "Download"
   - File type: JPG
   - Quality: Standard
   - Download

9. **Repeat for Twitter**
   - File > Resize > 1200 x 675 px
   - Adjust layout if needed
   - Download as twitter-image.jpg

## üí° PRO TIPS

1. **Use High-Quality Photos**
   - Minimum 1920x1080px source
   - Well-lit, professional looking
   - Shows your best products

2. **Keep It Simple**
   - Don't overcrowd with text
   - Focus on 1 main message
   - Use hierarchy (big text > small text)

3. **Test on Mobile**
   - View on phone screen
   - Text should still be readable
   - Logo should be recognizable

4. **Update Seasonally**
   - Holiday versions (Christmas, New Year)
   - Summer specials
   - Special events

5. **A/B Test**
   - Try different backgrounds
   - Test different text
   - See which gets more clicks
